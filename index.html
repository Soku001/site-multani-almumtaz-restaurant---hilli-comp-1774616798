Our Culinary Philosophy

At Multani AlMumtaz Restaurant, we are passionate about bringing the rich, vibrant culinary traditions of Pakistan to Al Ain. Every dish is a testament to time-honoured recipes, premium ingredients, and a dedication to authentic flavours. From the bustling streets of Lahore to the serene deserts of Al Ain, we invite you on a gastronomic journey.

Mouth-Watering Menu Highlights

Royal Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice cooked with tender marinated meat, aromatic spices, and a touch of saffron, a true feast for the senses.

Lahori Karahi

A rich, spicy stir-fried curry with succulent chicken or lamb, fresh tomatoes, ginger, and green chilies, cooked to perfection.

Authentic Seekh Kebab

Skewered minced meat, seasoned with traditional spices, grilled to smoky perfection, tender and bursting with flavour.

Nihari Delights

Slow-cooked beef or lamb stew, a rich and hearty dish with complex spices, served with fresh ginger and coriander.

Haleem Special

A wholesome and nutritious blend of wheat, barley, lentils, and meat, slow-cooked to a thick, paste-like consistency.

Chargrilled Tikka

Tender chicken or lamb pieces marinated in yogurt and spices, char-grilled to juicy perfection.

Meet Our Master Chef
